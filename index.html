The Man Of Steel

Superman is the most powerful being on planet Earth, an alien immigrant named Kal-El from the planet Krypton who was raised in Smallville, Kansas, with high moral ideals, he uses his incredible strengt, speed, flight and various other superpowers to fight evil, protect the innocent and stand as a shining beacon of hope. Superman is the ultimate embodiment of the American dream. Proof that an immigrant can come to a new land and achieve the greatest in human potential. He is charged to continually see the good in people and to keep on doing good when others don't see it. He does have this vulnerable, fumbling, geeky side that everybody has. And yet, he has this heroic, strong appearance as well. And it's something that people can relate to and can look up to.
